Q2. What is the salary of a Job?

The salary of a Job depends on your experience and qualification. The average salary for a job ranges from RS 49,999 to Rs 10,000,000 per year in India.

Q3. How can I apply for jobs on the PlacementIndia.com?

It is simple to find jobs on PlacementIndia.com.
  • Click on Apply Now button from job details page or job classified page
  • Register your self as a job seeker by providing some basic details
  • Add your updated resume for processing of the jobs
  • And track the responses in your mail and my folder after registration process.
